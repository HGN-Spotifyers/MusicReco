Methods
Our proposal is to develop a model which can predict songs in a more human manner. Reviewing the literature for recommender systems uncovered collaborative filtering as a methodology for representing user preferences and incorporating the human feedback elements into a model. Non-negative Matrix Factorization (NMF) is one known method for performing this type of collaborative filtering but any form of decomposition on the song-playlist space (such as PCA) could also be used to incorporate user feedback elements. To test our hypothesis that modelling based on implicit user preferences will outperform one based on attributes alone, we will build and test the following models and also propose an innovative method for song ordering and selection when a pool of valid songs has been found by one of the models.
